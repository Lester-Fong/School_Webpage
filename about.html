About Us

TRIMEX Colleges (formerly TRIMEX Institute of Science and Technology) is a private, higher educational institute in Bi√±an, Laguna. It was founded in 1994 and was initially a center for technical and short-term courses.

With the passion to reach out and make a difference through education, Ms. Fleurdeliz A. Constantino, the founder, spearheaded the operations of TRIMEX with Ms. Rachael C. Cruz in June 1994. Operation is composed of five (5) distinct departments namely Administration, Registrar, Accounting, Information Technology, and Secretarial department. The school started accepting students on a monthly basis offering the following courses: Two- Year Hardware Technology: Two-Year Software Technology; Two-Year Secretarial Course. A total of seventy four (74) students enrolled between June and July.
